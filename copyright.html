How We Use Content

Wallpapers on AnimeWallpaperz.in are shared under the following guidelines:

Note: Downloading wallpapers from our site is permitted for personal use only. Redistribution, commercial use, or modification of any third-party artwork without explicit permission from the original rights holder is strictly prohibited.

DMCA Takedown Process

If you are a copyright holder or authorized representative and believe that content on AnimeWallpaperz.in infringes your copyright, you may submit a DMCA takedown notice. Your notice must include:

  • Your full name and contact information (email & phone).
  • Identification of the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed.
  • The specific URL(s) on our site where the infringing content appears.
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner.
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Send your DMCA takedown notice to:

dmca@animewallpaperz.in

We process legitimate takedown requests within 72 hours of receipt. Content will be removed promptly while the matter is under review.

Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was removed by mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notice. The counter-notice must include your contact information, identification of the removed material, a statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake, and your consent to jurisdiction of the Indian courts.

Counter-notices should be sent to legal@animewallpaperz.in.

Some content on AnimeWallpaperz.in may be shared under the principles of fair use / fair dealing for purposes of commentary, criticism, fan appreciation, and non-commercial personal enjoyment. We respect intellectual property rights and work collaboratively with rights holders to resolve any concerns quickly and amicably.
